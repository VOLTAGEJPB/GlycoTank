Welcome to GlycoTank

Glycogen is stored carbohydrate in your muscles and liver—your short-term fuel.

  • It refills when you eat carbs and empties with training and daily activity.
  • Low tank often feels like low energy, brain fog, or carb cravings; high tank supports performance.
  • It changes hour-to-hour—this is not body fat, it’s fuel.

We track that “fuel tank” so your day makes more sense.

Knowing your tank level helps you plan food, training, and recovery.

  • Weight loss: Expect more hunger on low-tank days; hit protein/fiber to stay fuller in a deficit.
  • Smarter carbs: Aim most carbs around harder sessions; lighter days need less.
  • Better sessions: Avoid back-to-back “empty tank” training if quality matters.

Small, consistent choices beat guesswork and swings.

  • Meals add ≈ carbs × uptake% (default 85%).
  • Workouts subtract sets × set-cost (default 6g) and cardio g/min × intensity.
  • The chart shows trends; the Smart Carb Planner suggests carbs for tomorrow.
  • Personalize cap/uptake/costs anytime. No accounts; export whenever.

You’re in control—log a meal or set and watch the tank respond.
